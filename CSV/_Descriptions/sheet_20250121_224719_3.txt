This CSV file contains agricultural data detailing the cultivation of various fruit types, including strawberries, apples, and blueberries. Key attributes include planting and yield tonnage, yield quality, growing zone, weather conditions, soil pH, irrigation type, disease incidents, labor hours, costs, market prices, export percentages, storage losses, organic certification, processing grades, ripeness indices, and pest control methods. The data is timestamped with a date of January 2024, providing insights into fruit farming practices, environmental impacts, and economic factors in crop production.
